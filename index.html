About Salamati

Saving Lives Through Intelligent Vehicle Monitoring.

Road accidents become more dangerous when emergency teams receive the alert too late. Salamati focuses on the critical first minutes after a crash by connecting vehicle sensing, automatic alerts, and responder coordination in one system.

The hardware device continuously analyzes accelerometer and gyroscope data. When a collision pattern is detected, the platform confirms the case, shares the accident location, alerts the nearest paramedic, and notifies the family automatically.

Key Features

Everything Needed to Respond Faster

Six tightly integrated components working in concert to detect accidents, coordinate emergency services, and keep families informed.

Automatic Accident Detection

Multi-axis accelerometer and gyroscope fusion algorithms detect collision signatures in under 2 seconds, even at low speeds.

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Precision location data is captured the moment an accident occurs and streamed live to emergency services and family.

Emergency Dispatch

Automatic identification and alerting of the nearest available paramedic unit, reducing critical response time dramatically.

Driver Mobile App

Allows the driver to confirm or cancel an alert, trigger a manual SOS, view device status, and access emergency contacts instantly.

Paramedic Mobile App

Dedicated interface for paramedics showing live accident locations, patient details, route navigation, and incident history.

Family Notifications

Instant push alerts to pre-registered family contacts with the driver's live location and accident severity status.
